About Sangeetam

"Sangeetam 2024," the eagerly awaited annual cultural extravaganza of Shobhit Deemed University, goes beyond a mere celebration of art and culture. It embodies unity, diversity, and limitless creativity.With the theme "Harmonizing Cultures, Amplifying Talents," the festival signifies a dedication to fostering mutual respect, cross-cultural understanding, and the celebration of multifaceted talents. Its core objective is to weave a vibrant tapestry of cultures, offering a dynamic platform for students to express their identities, showcase their talents, and nurture innovative ideas.

This event is more than a festival; it's an opportunity for everyone to find belonging amidst the rich mosaic of cultures that enrich the university.

The diverse program includes performances, art exhibitions, interactive sessions, fashion showcases, entrepreneurial insights, literary evenings, and a star-studded finale, aiming to unify, deepen understanding, and encourage collaboration among students.

Ultimately, "Sangeetam 2024" reflects the university's commitment to a thriving, harmonious, and culturally enriched community, inviting all to join in celebrating the beauty of diverse cultures and talents.

About Shobhit University

Shobhit University believes in strategic growth as envisaged in our mission and vision, to impart quality education through vertical and horizontal integration. By fostering quality education, research and innovation, the University endeavors to empower youth.

The University translates its vision into reality on the path of achieving excellence in engineering, technology and management, based on the fundamentals of 21 century education to conform and serve basic human needs, for overall development and inclusive growth.
